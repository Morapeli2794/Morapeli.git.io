Background & Education

  • Morapeli Sekamane is a skilled Mechanical Technician currently working at UniPod, where he implements SOPs, enforces safety protocols, supports innovation projects, and handles machinery maintenance. Previously, he served as a Trade Instructor at Lerotholi Polytechnic, teaching fitting and machining, supervising workshops, and preparing students for industry. His earlier roles include Plant Engineer at AfriSam Lesotho, overseeing operations and SHE compliance, and Production Manager at Loti Brick, where he led production planning and quality control. He also held roles as a Maintenance Technician and Packaging Team Leader, gaining hands-on experience in mechanical assembly, machining, and team leadership. His technical strengths include mechanical maintenance, SOP development, project coordination, SAP proficiency, and curriculum development.
  • Morapeli Sekamane holds a B-Tech in Operations Management from Tshwane University of Technology (2005–2006), where he studied subjects such as Operations Management IV, Financial Planning and Control, and Marketing. He also earned a National Diploma in Production Management from Vaal University of Technology (2002–2004), focusing on production techniques, organizational effectiveness, and manufacturing engineering. His technical foundation was built at Lerotholi Polytechnic, where he obtained certificates in Fitting and Machining (1996–1999) and Plumbing (1997–1998), covering trade technology, CNC, hydraulics, and practical skills. He completed his secondary education at Khubetsoana High School with five passes at the C.O.S.C Ordinary level.
